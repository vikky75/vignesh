Jatayu earth's center

You will certainly get mesmerised with Kollam’s latest tourist attraction, the Jatayu Earth’s Center that hosts the world’s largest bird sculpture. Spanning over 65 acres across four hills, this is the State’s first ever BOT (Build-Operate-Transfer) tourism.

Thangassery-Lighthouse

The Tangasseri Lighthouse or Thangassery as it is locally called is the tallest lighthouse on the Kerala coast standing at 135 feet. It was built in 1902 and is easily one of the most picturesque locations in the region and sees a lot of travelers.
